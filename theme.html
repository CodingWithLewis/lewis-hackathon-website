Theme

Functionally Dysfunctional

We are looking for people to be creative by building a project that is functional but has some sort of strange unconventional thing about it.”

Think: “If it’s stupid but it works… it isn’t stupid.

Some Examples:

A video player that only plays video when you balance the bar.

A captcha that forces you to play minesweeper to pass.

A terminal application that forces the player to think in strange ways.
